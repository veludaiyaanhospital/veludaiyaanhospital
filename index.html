About Veludaiyaan Hospital

Advanced orthopedic & trauma care in Cuddalore

Veludaiyaan Ortho & Trauma Speciality Hospital is a dedicated center for advanced orthopedic, trauma, and joint care in Cuddalore. We combine clinical expertise, modern surgical techniques, and patient-centered treatment to ensure safe and effective recovery.

We are committed to ethical, transparent, and evidence-based orthopedic care for patients of all age groups.

Services

Premium structured specialties

📅 Book Appointment

🦴 Knee Joint Replacement

Advanced total and partial knee replacement surgeries to relieve severe arthritis pain and restore joint function.

🦴 Hip Surgery

Management of hip fractures, arthritis, and hip replacement procedures to improve mobility and independence.

🦴 Revision Surgery

Specialized care for failed or worn-out joint replacements using advanced revision techniques.

🧠 Spine Surgery

Treatment for slip disc, spinal injuries, degenerative disorders, and chronic back pain using modern techniques.

💪 Sports Injury & Rehabilitation

Management of ACL, PCL, meniscus tears, ligament injuries, and structured rehabilitation programs.

💪 Advanced Shoulder & Elbow Surgery

Treatment of rotator cuff injuries, frozen shoulder, fractures, dislocations, and complex elbow injuries.

🚑 Trauma Care (24×7)

Emergency management for road traffic accidents, complex fractures, dislocations, and polytrauma cases.

👶 Children Orthopaedics

Treatment of congenital deformities, pediatric fractures, and growth-related bone conditions.

🦶 Foot & Ankle Surgery

Management of deformities, fractures, ligament injuries, diabetic foot complications, and ankle instability.

🦷 Facio Maxillary Surgery

Advanced care for facial fractures, jaw injuries, and trauma-related facial conditions.

🧠 Plastic & Neuro Surgery Support

Collaborative surgical care for trauma-related soft tissue injuries and nerve conditions.

🦴 Arthritis Management

Comprehensive medical and surgical treatment for osteoarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, and degenerative joint diseases.

✨ Plastic Surgery

Reconstructive and aesthetic plastic surgery to restore form and function in areas affected by trauma, disease, or developmental conditions.

💉 Pain Medicine

Interdisciplinary chronic pain care combining pain medicine, physical rehabilitation, and guided pain-coping strategies to improve daily function and quality of life.

❤️ Cardio Care

Comprehensive heart care with risk-factor control, medications, and timely referral for cardiac procedures when needed, with a strong focus on long-term prevention.
